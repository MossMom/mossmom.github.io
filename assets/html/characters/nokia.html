Nokia

Nokia (any/all) is an aarakocra (bird-person) bard/artificer that specializes in alchemy and acid/poison based magic. He's a mischevious grafiti artist and often uses his ability of flight to put art in hard to reach/clean places.

He's a college of creation bard, with his signature paint-stained waistpiece, which is a stolen noble's silk. He also sports a celestial patterned cloth over his left shoulder to honor a fallen party member.

Due to extensive exposure to loud noises/explosions, he develops hearing loss that eventually becomes full deafness. He later learns sign langauge and utilizes telepathy to communicate with others.
