Ecstasy

Ecstasy (he/they) is a transmasc tiefling with pitch black eyes, a trait shared amongst most Cepheus family members. He was the older of his identical twin sister, one twin is born with a sun birthmark (him), the other with a moon birthmark (his sister).

The Cepheus family is cursed to always and only have 2 twins, each marked by their respective celestial body. The sun child is destined to inherit the power and fortune of the family, and for the past hundred generations, this has been the case.

X was born with the sun birthmark, but never wanted to become the head of the family, so he ran away before his 18th birthday, met a hag, and traded his valuable name for fame and power.

He took on his stage name, Ecstasy, and went on to become one of the most popular artists of his time, known throughout the planescape for his punk rock music.

This piece is a scene from the campaign, where X put on an impromptu concert to distract the enemies while the rest of the party planted bombs in their headquarters.

The antagonists of the DND campaign he's from are a coven of abyssal space witches, with the goal to unleash a trapped god of the abyss. They also torture/experiment on living things, so rest assured they deserved getting their headquarters blown up.

This is art of what his guitar and magic look like. He has an electric wisp familiar powering it, so he doesn't need to carry around an amp or speakers to be heard.

The guitar itself is a reflavored Lyre of Building, which has been been used extensively in the DND campaign he's from, mostly to prevent damage to structures and weapons, excavate tunnels, and create statues of fallen party members.

The deal with the hag gave him incredibly potent fey magic, which is reflected in the pinks and blues of his magic. This magic manifests mostly in his bard class, he's specifically a college of glamour bard.
