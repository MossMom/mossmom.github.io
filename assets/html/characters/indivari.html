Indivari

Indivari (she/her) is an Eladrin elf & battle smith artificer. A battle smith artificer is an arcane engineer who specializes in magical weapons, armor, and constructs. She's made two constructs (basically robots) that are always by her side. The constructs and her inventions have visual themes inspired by the solar punk aesthetic with stained glass detailing.

Indi lived in the Feywild with her family for most of her life, only leaving at 100 years old, the age of maturity for her people. Her later years at home were spent as an apprentice under various artificers. For eight years after this, she explored the material plane, and in the final of the eight years, came across the forgotten art of runesmithing, invented by giants.

Combined with her knowledge of artificing, this unique approach has allowed her to create powerful and innovative devices.

Giant runes draw magic and power from the natural world, in a similar way as how solar and wind energy draws from nature. Upon discovering these techniques, she saw their potential if combined with science, specifically artificing. After cataloging every rune she could find, she then went to the prestigious artificer guild of _____ to further her studies. Over the course of 30 years, she honed her skills and developed her own unique approach to artificing, one that utilized runic magic.

During her travels, she saw how the world was headed towards industrialization, and sought to create a more sustainable future by integrating magical and technological advancements. Elves live for a very long time, often only dying of age at 850 years. Indi believes that in her long lifetime, she can bring about the sustainable, natural future she envisions. She plans to dedicate the rest of her life to this cause, for both her people and the entire world.

All of her creations reflect her commitment to sustainability and the harmonious integration of magic and technology. Her spells are all reimagined through the lens of environmental consciousness, reflavored to fit her eco-friendly approach. The full descriptions of all her devices can be found in her notes document.

Not only does she create and utilize eco-friendly technologies, but she's also dabbled in magical constructs which assist her and her party in both combat and exploration. First is Trynket, a small robot hummingbird with stained glass wings. Trynket assists with any skill checks the party needs to make, whether that means searching a room for hidden compartments or providing support in identifying magical or natural phenomena. It communicates in a melodic chirping sound, which Indi, or anyone familiar with Trynket, can translate into words. This construct is actually a reflavoring of the cantrip Guidance, which provides a bonus 1d4 to any chosen skill checks.

Second is __Name__, a robot kingfisher (with stained glass wings again) which mostly assists in combat though its own attacks and disruption of enemy actions. Its abilities and stats are determined by the artificer subclass of Battle Smith, as this construct is Indi's Steel Defender. it pecks with its metal beak, and refracts light through its stained glass wings to temporarily blind attackers. Both constructs were created while she worked at the artificer guild.
