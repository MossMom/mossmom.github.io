Mossy

She/They/Fae

Hi! You probably already know me, but I go by both Mossy or Raine, no preference between the two. I am the host of my 'system'The collection of all alters within a person's mind., which means I spend the most time 'fronting'The person currently in control of the body, multiple different alters can front at once, which is called 'cofronting'. out of everyone.

While I am the host, I have very little awareness and control over the internal 'headspace'The mental space where an individual's different alters reside, it can function as a separate realm within the mind, and looks/functions uniquely for each system. of my system. I believe there may be more alters than the three with their own pages here, but I'm ultimately unsure. I have constant memory gaps, a common symptom of DID, so I often rely on the persistence of digital communication so that I can go back and refresh myself on previous conversations and events, something I cannot do with verbal communication, unless I take notes, which I don't often do.

I am quite extroverted and love socializing and meeting new people, and I am very open about my mental health and my experiences with DID, if you have any questions feel free to ask! A fitting animal to describe me would be a maine coon calico, and a fitting mythical archetype would be a fae.

My hobbies mostly include gaming, both tabletop and video, as well as story/character writing, and other computer science related things such as game development and programming. I've learned some American Sign Language and can (slowly) communicate through it, however I am far from fluent, my expressive skills are much better than my receptive.

I've been through a lot of hard times. But I'm still here. And I'm still trying.
