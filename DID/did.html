Dissociative Identity Disorder

Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID) is a mental health condition where an individual experiences the presence of two or more distinct identities, also called 'alters' within their mind. Each alter may act and think extremely differently from each other, and sometimes hold their own unique memories, behaviors, and skills. The personality that spends the most time in control is typically called the 'host', I, Mossy, am the host of my 'system'The collection of all alters within a person's mind..

I have been diagnosed with DID, and have three other alters that I am aware of, however it is very likely there are more, as I feel extremely disconnected from my system, and the internal 'headspace'The mental space where an individual's different alters reside, it can function as a separate realm within the mind, and looks/functions uniquely for each system. they reside in.

The other alters I am aware of are Ophelia, Everest, and Myx. I have created separate pages for each of them, as well as myself, which can be accessed through the additional navigation bar above.
